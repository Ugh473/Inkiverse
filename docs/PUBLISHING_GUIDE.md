## Getting Started

### Before You Start

1. Create and verify your Inkiverse account
2. Complete your author profile
3. Review content guidelines and community standards
4. Prepare your content in the appropriate format

### Author Profile Setup

- **Display Name**: Your pen name or author name
- **Bio**: Brief description (max 500 characters)
- **Avatar**: Profile picture (recommended: 500x500px)
- **Links**: Website, social media, portfolio links
- **Genres**: Select your primary genres

## Creating a Series

### Basic Information

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Title** | Unique, descriptive series name |
| **Description** | 50-500 character synopsis |
| **Cover Image** | 300x450px JPG/PNG (required) |
| **Content Type** | Comic, Novel, Light Novel, Manga, Manwha, Manhua |
| **Genres** | Select up to 5 categories |
| **Target Audience** | Age rating (All Ages, Teen, Mature, Adult) |
| **Status** | Ongoing, Completed, Hiatus |
| **Language** | Publication language |

### Content Warnings

Clearly mark any sensitive content:

- Violence or graphic content
- Sexual content
- Drugs or substance abuse
- Mental health themes
- Flashing/seizure warnings
- Other trigger warnings

### Series Settings

- **Update Schedule**: Regular publication dates help build audience
- **Subscription Model**: Free, Paid, or Freemium
- **Comments**: Enable/disable community feedback
- **Ratings**: Allow community ratings and reviews

## Content Type Guides

### 📖 Novels

**Format**: Text-based long-form narrative

**Specifications**:
- Plain text or Rich text formatting
- Chapter length: 1,000-20,000+ words recommended
- Supported formats: .txt, .docx, .pdf (converted to web format)
- Images: Optional illustrations/chapter art

**Best Practices**:
- Clear chapter titles and organization
- Consistent formatting and typography
- Table of Contents for long works
- Author's notes for context

**Upload Process**:
1. Create new chapter
2. Paste or upload text content
3. Add chapter-specific metadata
4. Preview before publishing
5. Schedule or publish immediately

---

### 🎨 Comics

**Format**: Panel-based visual storytelling

**Specifications**:
- Image size: 1280x1920px minimum (vertical layout) or 1920x1280px (horizontal)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- File size: Under 5MB per page
- Resolution: 300 DPI recommended for print quality

**Panel Layout**:
- Clear panel divisions
- Readable dialogue and captions
- Consistent art style throughout
- Quality color work or clean line art

**Upload Process**:
1. Prepare high-quality page images
2. Create new chapter
3. Upload pages in order
4. Add dialogue/translation if needed
5. Set reading direction (left-to-right or right-to-left)
6. Preview and publish

---

### 📚 Light Novels

**Format**: Text with illustrations (Japanese-style)

**Specifications**:
- Primary content: Novel text (similar to novels guide)
- Illustrations: 1-3 images per chapter recommended
- Cover art: Illustrated (specific to light novel style)
- Text length: 3,000-10,000 words per chapter typical

**Illustration Guidelines**:
- Character artwork and key scenes
- Color or high-quality black & white
- 1000x1500px minimum image size
- Credits/artist information required

**Best Practices**:
- Maintain consistent tone and style
- Use illustrations to enhance key moments
- Include author's afterword/notes
- Provide context for series universe

---

### 🗾 Manga

**Format**: Japanese comic format with sequential art

**Specifications**:
- Traditional right-to-left reading direction
- Standard page size: 1280x1920px (portrait)
- Recommended resolution: 300 DPI
- Double-page spreads: 2560x1920px
- Format: JPG or PNG

**Manga-Specific Elements**:
- Japanese reading direction (automatic reversal option)
- Sound effects and onomatopoeia
- Manga screentones and effects
- Clean inking and line work
- Quality halftone gradations

**Quality Standards**:
- Crisp, clear artwork
- Proper contrast and readability
- Consistent paneling and flow
- Professional or semi-professional quality

---

### 🇰🇷 Manwha

**Format**: Korean comic format

**Specifications**:
- Vertical webtoon format: 800x1200px pages (or 800x6000px for full scrolling episodes)
- Alternative format: Traditional horizontal pages (1280x1920px)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Color or high-quality grayscale

**Manwha-Specific Features**:
- Vertical scroll reading (optional webtoon format)
- Modern visual effects
- Contemporary art style (typically)
- Digital-first design

---

### 🇨🇳 Manhua

**Format**: Chinese comic format

**Specifications**:
- Standard page size: 1280x1920px
- Alternative: Vertical scroll format (800x varying)
- Left-to-right reading direction
- Format: JPG or PNG
- 300 DPI recommended

**Manhua-Specific Elements**:
- Chinese reading direction (left-to-right modern, right-to-left classical)
- Art style (contemporary or traditional)
- Dialogue in Chinese
- Cultural context and storytelling

## Chapter Management

### Publishing a Chapter

1. **Create Chapter**
   - Add chapter number and title
   - Write or upload content
   - Add chapter description (optional)

2. **Add Metadata**
   - Content warnings specific to chapter
   - Release date/time
   - Chapter art or thumbnail

3. **Preview**
   - Verify formatting and display
   - Check all images/content
   - Test reading experience

4. **Publish**
   - Immediate publication or schedule
   - Notify subscribers
   - Share on social media

### Chapter Settings

- **Status**: Draft, Scheduled, Published, Archived
- **Visibility**: Public, Subscribers Only, Private
- **Comments**: Enabled/Disabled
- **Monetization**: Free or Premium chapter

### Organization

- Logical chapter numbering (1, 2, 3... or 1.1, 1.2...)
- Volume grouping for long series
- Arc or part organization for complex works
- Clear progression and pacing

## Publishing Standards

### Content Quality

- **Text Content**: Proper grammar, spelling, and formatting
- **Visual Content**: High resolution, clear and legible
- **Consistency**: Uniform quality across chapters
- **Professionalism**: Publication-ready presentation

### Copyright & Attribution

- Original work or proper licensing rights
- Image sources credited
- Collaboration credits clearly noted
- Trademark and brand usage respected

### Metadata Best Practices

- **Title**: Clear, searchable, appealing
- **Description**: Engaging synopsis without spoilers
- **Tags**: 5-10 relevant, searchable tags
- **Categories**: Accurate genre classification
- **Warnings**: All content warnings clearly listed

### Cover Art

- Professional or semi-professional quality
- Reflects content accurately
- Proper dimensions (300x450px standard)
- Readable title and author name (if included)

## Monetization

### Free vs. Paid Models

| Model | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Free** | All chapters accessible to all users |
| **Paid** | Series requires purchase or subscription |
| **Freemium** | Recent chapters free; older chapters paid |
| **Premium** | Free to read; optional patron support |

### Earning Options

1. **Reader Subscriptions**: Monthly access to your content
2. **Chapter Sales**: Individual chapter purchases
3. **Patron Program**: Support from dedicated fans
4. **Ad Revenue**: Revenue from platform advertising
5. **Merchandise**: Sale of physical copies (future feature)

### Best Practices for Success

- **Consistent Updates**: Regular publication schedule
- **Quality Content**: Invest in your craft
- **Engage Community**: Respond to comments and feedback
- **Social Media**: Promote your work
- **Free Content Strategy**: Build audience with free chapters, monetize premium content
