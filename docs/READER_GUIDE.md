## Reading Content

### Starting a Series

1. **Visit Series Page**
   - Read synopsis and reviews
   - Check content warnings
   - View author information
   - See chapter list

2. **Choose Reading Format**
   - Web reader (built-in)
   - Full-screen mode
   - Device-specific format

3. **Start Reading**
   - Begin with Chapter 1
   - Or jump to any chapter
   - Continue from where you left off

### Reading Interfaces

#### Novel Reader

- **Font Controls**: Size, style, spacing, color
- **Background**: Light/dark/sepia modes
- **Pacing**: Read at your speed
- **Bookmarks**: Save important passages
- **Notes**: Add personal annotations
- **Dictionary**: Hover definitions (where available)

#### Comic Reader

- **Zoom**: Fit page, fit width, fit height, or zoom manually
- **Navigation**: Next/previous page
- **Reading Direction**: Automatic or manual left-to-right/right-to-left
- **Double-Page View**: View spreads together
- **Fullscreen**: Immersive reading mode

#### Manga/Manwha/Manhua Reader

- **Page Fit**: Multiple fit options
- **Reading Direction**: Set for series or override per chapter
- **Continuous Scroll**: Read without page breaks (manwha style)
- **Traditional Paging**: Jump between pages
- **Effects**: Optimize for screentones and effects

### Reading Progress

- **Automatic Tracking**: Your position saves automatically
- **Reading List**: Series you're currently reading
- **Continue Reading**: Jump back to where you left off
- **Mark Complete**: Mark series as finished
- **Progress Bar**: See how far through the chapter/series you are

### Offline Reading

(Feature availability depends on series license)

- Download chapters to read without internet
- Available on mobile apps
- Automatic sync when online
- Storage management controls

## Community Features

### Reviews & Ratings

**Reading Reviews**:
- See what other readers think
- Filter by rating (1-5 stars)
- Sort by helpful, recent, popular
- Find spoiler-free reviews

**Writing Reviews**:
- Rate from 1-5 stars
- Write your thoughts (optional)
- Mark as spoiler (hides content)
- Explain what you liked/disliked
- Help other readers discover great content

**Review Etiquette**:
- Be respectful and constructive
- Avoid major spoilers
- Critique content, not creators
- One review per series

### Comments & Discussion

**Chapter Comments**:
- Comment on specific chapters
- Reply to other readers
- React with emoji
- Report inappropriate comments
- Creator responses welcome!

**Series Discussion**:
- Dedicated discussion thread
- Theories and predictions
- Fan art and creations
- Community events
- Author participation

**Community Guidelines**:
- Be respectful and kind
- No spoilers in comments without warning
- No spam or self-promotion
- Follow content policies
- Report harassment

### Following Authors

**Why Follow?**:
- Get notified of new chapters
- See all an author's works
- Receive update announcements
- Access exclusive content (if available)
- Show your support

**Managing Follows**:
- View authors you follow
- Mute notifications (temporarily)
- Unfollow anytime
- Direct author messaging (where available)

### User Interaction

**Profiles**:
- See what others are reading
- Check their reviews and ratings
- View their lists and recommendations
- Follow other readers

**Messaging**:
- Message readers and authors
- Create reading groups
- Discuss series privately
- Share recommendations

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I read offline?**  
A: Some content supports offline reading. Look for the download option on chapters.

**Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**  
A: Use the report button on any series or comment. Our moderation team will review.

**Q: Can I send direct messages to authors?**  
A: Authors can enable or disable messaging. Check their profile settings.

**Q: How often does content update?**  
A: Update frequency depends on the creator. Check series page for schedule.

**Q: Can I export my library or reviews?**  
A: Yes, check account settings for export options.
