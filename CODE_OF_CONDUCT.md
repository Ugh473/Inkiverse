# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Inkiverse is dedicated to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive community for all members - whether you're a writer, reader, developer, or fan. We are committed to:

- Creating a welcoming environment for people of all backgrounds
- Treating everyone with respect and dignity
- Fostering creative expression and diverse perspectives
- Maintaining a safe space free from harassment and discrimination

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all members with kindness and respect, regardless of age, gender, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, religion, disability, or any other characteristic
- **Be Constructive**: Provide thoughtful, constructive feedback that helps others grow
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and experiences
- **Respect Boundaries**: Honor others' privacy and personal boundaries
- **Follow Guidelines**: Adhere to content guidelines, copyright laws, and community standards
- **Report Issues**: Notify moderators of violations or concerning behavior

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
- Discrimination based on identity or personal characteristics
- Sexual harassment, unwanted advances, or inappropriate content
- Hate speech or slurs of any kind
- Threats, doxxing, or targeting individuals
- Spam or coordinated harassment campaigns
- Copyright infringement or intellectual property violations
- Impersonation or deceptive practices
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Violent content or glorification of violence (outside appropriate fictional contexts)

## Content Standards

While Inkiverse supports diverse creative expression:

- All adult content must be appropriately labeled with clear warnings
- Content must not violate copyright or intellectual property rights
- Illegal content will not be tolerated
- Extreme violence or graphic content must be clearly marked
- Child safety is paramount; content sexualizing minors is strictly prohibited

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document** the incident (screenshots, links, timestamps)
2. **Report** to moderators via:
   - Abuse report button on content
   - Direct message to moderators
   - Email: conduct@inkiverse.example.com
3. **Provide Context**: Include as much detail as possible

### Response Process

- Reports are reviewed promptly and confidentially
- Moderators investigate and contact relevant parties
- Actions may include warnings, content removal, or account suspension
- Persistent violations result in permanent bans
- Appeals process available for major actions

## Consequences

Violations may result in:

- **Warning**: First-time minor violations
- **Content Removal**: Inappropriate content is removed
- **Temporary Suspension**: 24-72 hour account suspension
- **Account Suspension**: Extended suspension (30+ days)
- **Permanent Ban**: Removal from platform for serious or repeated violations

## Creator Responsibilities

Creators have additional responsibilities:

- **Content Warnings**: Properly tag and warn about sensitive content
- **Accurate Descriptions**: Honestly describe your work
- **Respond Professionally**: Handle criticism and feedback maturely
- **Moderate Comments**: Address inappropriate content in your own series
- **Respect Feedback**: Consider constructive criticism constructively

## Moderator Standards

Moderators are held to the highest standards:

- Act impartially and fairly
- Maintain confidentiality of reports
- Provide clear explanations for actions taken
- Recuse themselves from situations with conflicts of interest
- Lead by example

## Questions?

- Review this code of conduct regularly
- Ask moderators if you have questions
- Suggest improvements to guidelines
